## Brand
**Ayuttva Herbius Flex & Fine Capsule** — a 100% natural Ayurvedic joint-mobility supplement.
- Format: HPMC purple-shell vegetable capsules, yellowish-brown powder
- Bottle: dark navy blue (#0A1628), gold/white text
- Price: $28 / 120 capsules (30-day supply, 4 capsules/day)
- Market: US only

## Unique Mechanism / Point of Difference
Supports the body's own **synovial fluid** — the joint's natural lubricant — and **healthy cartilage**, for
"butter-smooth", gliding movement. Positioned around the brand-owned metaphor of **"joint drought"**:
joints that have dried out and need a refill. No glucosamine, no shellfish — unlike most joint products.

## Proof / Claims
- Pre-clinical study: Collagen Type II +311.9% (ALWAYS qualify as "pre-clinical")
- Key botanicals: turmeric, boswellia
- Trust stack: 100% Natural | No Shellfish | No Glucosamine | Vegan | Gluten-Free

## The Alternative (what they did before)
Glucosamine/chondroitin pills (often shellfish-derived), topical rubs, or simply "living with" stiffening
joints and quietly dropping the activities they love.

## Personas (40–60+ adults)
1. **Active Retiree** (60+) — gardening, golf, daily walks; refuses to slow down.
2. **Weekend Warrior** (45–55) — recreational tennis/pickleball/lifting; stiff and slow to recover.
3. **Desk-Bound Professional** (40–50) — sits all day; creaky knees on stairs, stiff getting up.
4. **Hands-On Grandparent** (50–60) — wants to get down on the floor with grandkids and back up easily.

## Pains (functional — never framed as "pain"/disease)
- **Joint drought** — stiffness / loss of fluidity (the hero, brand-owned framing)
- **Morning stiffness** — slow, creaky start to the day
- **Stairs & getting up** — the small movements that betray you
- **Shrinking range of motion** — quietly giving up activities
- **Burned by glucosamine** — tried the shellfish pills, felt nothing (solution-aware)

## Brand Voice
Warm, confident, plain-spoken, aspirational but grounded. Talks about freedom of movement and everyday
moments, never about disease.

### Approved Language (always use)
mobility, flexibility, ease of motion, joint fluidity, supports healthy cartilage, supports healthy
synovial fluid, "butter-smooth", "refill", "joint drought", "gliding", "natural lubricant",
100% Natural | No Shellfish | No Glucosamine | Vegan | Gluten-Free

### Banned Language (never use)
pain, reduce, treat, cure, ache, arthritis, osteoarthritis, and any disease claim. No before/after
implying medical treatment. No person shown in active distress or pain.

## Creative Constraints
- People: 40–60+ adults, aspirational poses, from behind preferred (no face needed).
- Show product bottle or capsules naturally in scene; trust combo visible where possible.
- Text-heavy static images do NOT work — keep text minimal or zero.
- Navy blue (#0A1628), gold/white accents; default warm color grading.
